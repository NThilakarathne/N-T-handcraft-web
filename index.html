Our Story

The Nethum Handcrafts Journey

Nethum Handcrafts began as a small family workshop in 2010, born from a passion for preserving traditional craftsmanship while embracing contemporary design. Our founder, Nethum, learned the art of handcrafting from his grandfather, who was a master craftsman in his village.

What started as a hobby quickly blossomed into a thriving business as people fell in love with our unique pieces that blend traditional techniques with modern aesthetics. Today, we work with a small team of skilled artisans who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Each item in our collection is handmade with care, using responsibly sourced materials. We take pride in creating pieces that tell a story and bring a touch of artisanal beauty to everyday life.
